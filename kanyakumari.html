Let's Got To KANYAKUMARI

4-Day Travel Schedule – Kanyakumari

Day 1 – Arrival & Local Exploration

  1. Arrive in Kanyakumari and check into hotel
  2. Visit Kanyakumari Beach (meeting point of 3 seas)
  3. Explore Gandhi Memorial Mandapam
  4. Enjoy sunset view at Sunset Point

Day 2 –Spiritual And Cultural sights

  1. Early morning sunrise at the beach
  2. Visit Vivekananda Rock Memorial
  3. Thiruvalluvar Statue
  4. Kanyakumari Temple (Kumari Amman Temple)

Day 3 –Excursions Around Kanyakumari

  1. Padmanabhapuram Palace (historic wooden palace)
  2. Suchindram Temple (famous for musical pillars)
  3. Vattakottai Fort
  4. Evening leisure walk at the beach

Day 4 – Leisure & Return

  1. Morning visit to local handicraft shops
  2. Explore Tsunami Memorial Park
  3. Quick photography at View Tower
  4. Departure
