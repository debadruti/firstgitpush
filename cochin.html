Let's Got To COCHIN

4-Day Travel Schedule – Cochin (Kochi)

Day 1 – Arrival & Local Exploration

  1. Arrive in Kochi and check into hotel
  2. Visit Fort Kochi and see the Chinese Fishing Nets
  3. Walk around Jew Town & Mattancherry Palace
  4. Evening Kathakali dance show

Day 2 – Heritage And Culture

  1. St. Francis Church (oldest European church in India)
  2. Santa Cruz Basilica
  3. Kerala Folklore Museum
  4. Marine Drive in the evening

Day 3 –Backwaters and Nature

  1. Day trip to Alleppey (houseboat or backwater cruise)
  2. Enjoy Kerala cuisine on the boat
  3. Return to Kochi by evening

Day 4 – Leisure & Return

  1. Morning visit to Hill Palace Museum
  2. Shopping for spices, handicrafts, and souvenirs
  3. Relax at Cherai Beach (if time permits)
  4. Departure
