Let's Got To VALPARAI

4-Day Travel Schedule – Valparai

Day 1 – Arrival & Local Exploration

  1. Travel via Pollachi → Aliyar Dam → Hairpin bends scenic drive
  2. Stop at Aliyar Dam & Monkey Falls
  3. Check into hotel/homestay
  4. Evening walk in tea estates

Day 2 – Nature & Wildlife

  1. Sholayar Dam in the morning
  2. Nallamudi Viewpoint
  3. Indira Gandhi Wildlife Sanctuary / Anamalai Tiger Reserve
  4. Evening relaxation at stay

Day 3 – Waterfalls & Viewpoints

  1. Balaji Temple
  2. Birla Falls & Chinna Kallar Falls
  3. Grass Hills (with permission)
  4. Sunset at Loam’s View Point

Day 4 – Leisure & Return

  1. Morning walk in tea plantations
  2. Visit Tea Factory (if accessible)
  3. Local shopping (tea, coffee, honey)
  4. Return journey
